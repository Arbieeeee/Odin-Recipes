Rolo Pretzel Turtles

Description

This Rolo pretzel recipe is easy and fun to make with pretzels, caramel-covered chocolate candies, and pecan nuts for a delicious sweet treat. Store cooled turtles in an airtight container.

These pretzel turtles (a.k.a. pretzel Rolo pecan cookies) are a little bit sweet, a little bit salty, and 100% delicious.

Ingredients

  • Mini pretzels
  • chocolate-covered caramel candies
  • pecan halves

Steps

  1. Line an 8-inch square pan with aluminum foil; set aside.
  2. Arrange the pretzels on a baking sheet.
  3. Place a candy on each pretzel.
  4. Bake until the chocolate softens.
  5. Press a pecan half into each warm candy.
  6. Cool completely.
